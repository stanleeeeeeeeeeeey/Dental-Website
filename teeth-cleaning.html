Teeth Cleaning

Regular teeth cleaning is vital for maintaining oral health. Our professional dental cleanings remove plaque, tartar, and stains that brushing and flossing can't. Cleanings help prevent gum disease, cavities, and bad breath.

During your visit, our hygienists will examine your gums, clean your teeth with special tools, and offer tips for at-home care. Most patients benefit from a cleaning every 6 months.

Duration:

Approximately 30 to 60 minutes.

Recommended for:

  • All patients over 6 years old
  • People with braces or dental work
  • Patients with gum issues or halitosis
